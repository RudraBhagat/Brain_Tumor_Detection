1. Intended Use

This website provides AI-generated classification outputs from uploaded MRI images. These outputs should be used only as supplementary information.

2. Not a Medical Device Replacement

NeuroScan AI does not replace radiologist interpretation, neurologic examination, pathology confirmation, or multidisciplinary tumor board review.

All decisions related to diagnosis, prognosis, and treatment must be made by licensed clinicians with access to full patient context.

3. Emergency Situations

  • Do not delay emergency care while waiting for AI output.
  • Contact emergency services for severe or rapidly worsening symptoms.
  • Use hospital protocols and physician judgment as the primary source of care.

4. Clinical Best Practice

Correlate Clinically

Interpret AI output together with history, symptoms, and radiology findings.

Verify with Experts

Seek specialist review for uncertain or high-risk presentations.

Document Decisions

Record final clinical rationale independent of automated recommendations.
